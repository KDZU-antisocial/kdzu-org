# Video Component Example

This post shows how to use the Vimeo video component in your MDX content. You can embed videos with various customizations.

## Basic Video Embed

Here's a simple video embed:

<VimeoVideo videoId="148751763" />

## Customized Video

Here's a video with custom styling:

<VimeoVideo 
  videoId="148751763"
  color="#ff6b6b"
  title={false}
  byline={false}
  portrait={false}
/>

## Autoplay Video

This video will autoplay when the page loads (muted for browser compatibility):

<VimeoVideo 
  videoId="148751763"
  autoplay={true}
  muted={true}
  loop={true}
/>

## Video with Start Time

This video starts at 30 seconds:

<VimeoVideo 
  videoId="148751763"
  startTime={30}
/>

## Minimal Controls

A video with minimal player interface:

<VimeoVideo 
  videoId="148751763"
  controls={false}
  pip={false}
  title={false}
  byline={false}
  portrait={false}
/>

## Usage in Content

You can place videos anywhere in your content, and they'll be responsive and properly styled. The component automatically handles:

- Responsive design
- Accessibility features
- Custom styling options
- Performance optimization

The videos will work seamlessly with your existing content and styling.
